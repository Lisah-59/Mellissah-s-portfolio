Boiled Potatoes with Beef Stew and Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Beef (cut into pieces)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Garlic, ginger, and other spices
  • Salt and pepper
  • Water for stew

Instructions:

  1. Boil the potatoes until soft.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the beef pieces until browned.
  3. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, garlic, and spices to the beef. Let it cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add water and let it simmer until the beef is tender and the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve the beef stew with boiled potatoes and enjoy!
