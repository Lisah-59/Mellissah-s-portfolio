Fried Potatoes and Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Spices (paprika, garlic powder, etc.)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin slices, then fry until golden brown.
  2. Marinate the chicken with olive oil, lemon juice, and spices for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Grill the chicken on medium heat until cooked through and juicy.
  4. Serve the fried potatoes alongside the grilled chicken, and enjoy!
